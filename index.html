How we met

Our journey began during our fourth year of high school when we became classmates and typical friends. After graduation, we both embarked on the same journey to college, pursuing degrees in the field of information technology. It wasn't until our fourth year of college when we found ourselves in the same class once again, and that marked the beginning of something special. We got to know each other on a deeper level, and it wasn't long before we officially became a couple just before graduation. The rest, as they say, is history. Our relationship has been a beautiful and occasionally challenging five-year love story, but it's a journey we wouldn't trade for anything. This year, we got engaged, and we're looking forward to our upcoming wedding.
